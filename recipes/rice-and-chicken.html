Rice and Chicken

This Chicken and Rice Skillet is a quick and delicious one-pan dish that combines tender pieces of seasoned chicken with aromatic rice, saut√©ed vegetables, and a flavorful blend of herbs and spices. The result is a wholesome and satisfying meal that's perfect for busy weeknights or any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 1.5 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced (any color you prefer)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish (optional)

Method:

Prepare Rice:

Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.

In a separate pot, bring 2 cups of chicken broth to a boil.

Add the rinsed rice to the boiling broth, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

Cook Chicken:

Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, oregano, cumin, and paprika.

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.

Add the seasoned chicken and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through. This usually takes about 5-7 minutes.

Saute Vegetables:

Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper to the skillet. Saute until the vegetables are softened.

Combine Ingredients:

Stir in the cooked rice and diced tomatoes into the skillet with the chicken and vegetables. Mix well to combine.

Finish and Serve:

Adjust seasoning with additional salt and pepper if needed.

Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro if desired.

Serve hot and enjoy your simple and flavorful chicken and rice skillet!

Nutritional Information (per serving, approximately 4 servings):

  • Calories: ~400-450 kcal
  • Protein: ~30g
  • Carbohydrates: ~40g
  • Fat: ~12g
