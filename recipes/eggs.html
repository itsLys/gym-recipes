Scrambled Eggs

This Scrambled Eggs recipe is a quick and delightful dish that transforms simple eggs into a flavorful and satisfying meal. Perfect for a speedy breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chives or parsley for garnish (optional)

Method:

Preheat Pan:

Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

Whisk Eggs:

In a bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten. Season with salt and pepper.

Scramble:

Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, pushing them from the edges toward the center as they cook.

Cook to Desired Consistency:

Continue cooking and stirring until the eggs reach your preferred level of doneness. Be careful not to overcook.

Garnish and Serve:

Sprinkle with fresh chives or parsley if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious scrambled eggs!

Nutritional Information (per serving)

  • Calories: ~200 kcal
  • Protein: ~12g
  • Carbohydrates: ~1g
  • Fat: ~16g
